Vehicle tax and SORN

Before you contact DVLA, read how to:

  • tax your vehicle
  • make a Statutory Off Road Notification (SORN)
  • check if a vehicle is taxed
  • cancel vehicle tax and get a refund
  • pay your vehicle tax by Direct Debit
  • tax your vehicle without a V11 reminder
  • change your vehicle’s tax class

When to contact DVLA

It can take a few weeks to process applications. Wait for the processing time before contacting DVLA.

Application Processing time
Vehicle tax refund 6 weeks
Confirmation of SORN 4 weeks
